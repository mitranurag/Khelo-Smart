Abstract

The purpose of this project is to automate the existing manual system by the help of full-fledged website, fulfilling the requirements of the academy, so that their valuable data can be stored for a longer period with easy accessing and manipulation of the same. The management level, faculty and students to convey and retrieve information. Our project can lead to error free, secure, reliable and fast management system. The management level can access the information about the students. The faculty can convey any type of information. The students can pay fee through online payment and can receive information from the academy. Our project aims to provide a mobile system which can be accessed by the users anytime to convey or retrieve any information.

Existing system

The existing system has manual system. The staff have to maintain different types of records to store information. The whole process is very time consuming. The users have to visit the office to get any news or notification. The academy also has to rely on print media to inform students and trainers about any upcoming event.



Proposed System

The proposed system will automate the whole process of the academy. The trainer and students can easily access the website to get information. It will have a central server where all the data will be stored securely.The students will get all necessary updates easily and they can track their progress easily. They can do payment through online mode also. The trainer can update the student performance and schedule the time-slots easily.
